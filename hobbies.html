Hobbies

One of my favorite hobbies is photography. I love going outside and exploring nature, and I always bring my camera with me on my adventures. I use a Nikon D3500 with a stock lens and telephoto lens, and take shots of any size, from macro to landscape. Editing photos with Photoshop and Lightroom is especially fun for me, as the software opens up even more ways to make my shots look cool.
