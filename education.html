Education

I started my college career in 2018 at Lee University studying graphic design. Design was something I enjoyed for several years before studying it in college, and it still is. However, after a couple of years, I decided that while I enjoyed graphic design, it wasn't something I wanted to build a career out of.


I began looking for something else that I would enjoy as a career and that would also make use of my design skills, and discovered that web design/development was a great fit. I had already played around with HTML and CSS before when trying to build an online portfolio of my design work, so I went online to start learning more about it and found that I really enjoyed coding websites. I ended up choosing APSU as the place to continue my education because of the school's extensive computer science programs, several of which are offered online. I am currently in my second semester here at APSU, and am working towards a major in Computer Information Technology - Internet & Web Technology with a minor in Mobile Software Technology.


I have really enjoyed my time at APSU so far, and look forward to learning more about web and app development.
